Frequently asked questions

Is ChemBioCalc free?

Yes. ChemBioCalc is free to download and use for core tools such as the molarity, dilution, molar mass, pH and PCR calculators. An optional Pro subscription unlocks advanced analytical, molecular biology and pharmacology modules.

What can ChemBioCalc calculate?

ChemBioCalc covers 60+ calculations including molarity, dilution (C₁V₁ = C₂V₂), molar mass, pH and buffers, stoichiometry, titration, PCR/qPCR, CRISPR guide design, primer design, method validation (ICH Q2), HPLC, Design of Experiments (DoE/RSM), pharmacokinetics, IV drip rate and pediatric dose.

Does ChemBioCalc work offline?

Yes. ChemBioCalc works fully offline and requires no account — your data stays on your device.

Which platforms is ChemBioCalc available on?

ChemBioCalc is available for iOS on the App Store and for Android on Google Play, in English and Turkish.

Can I export my results?

Yes. Every result can be exported as a PDF or Excel report with the formulas and citations used.
