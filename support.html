Frequently Asked Questions

What calculators are included?

ChemBioCalc includes solution preparation, dilution, molarity, buffer, UV-Vis, molecular biology (PCR, gel), pharmacology (Km, IC50), unit conversion, colorimetry, and more.

Is my data stored online?

No. All calculations and notebook entries are stored locally on your device. No account required.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

The app crashed / I found a bug

Please contact us via email below with a short description of what happened. Include your device model and OS version if possible.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

ChemBioCalc ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application for chemistry and biology calculations. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

ChemBioCalc does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information. All calculations and notebook entries are stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

Camera & Photo Library

The app may request access to your camera and photo library solely for the RGB colorimetry feature (capturing or selecting images for color analysis). Images are processed on-device only and are never uploaded or stored by us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of ChemBioCalc displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – advertising
  • Apple App Store / Google Play – in-app purchases

Children's Privacy

ChemBioCalc is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
